Trish's Surf & Turf

It is great way to indulge for a special occasion or a date night.

Ingredients

  • 2 (8-12 ounce) filet mignon steaks
  • 4 lobster tails (8-10 ounces each)
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Lemons
  • Secret seasonings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375째. Split lobster tails in half lengthwise. With cut side up and using scissors, cut along the edge of shell to loosen the cartilage covering the tail meat from the shell; remove and discard cartilage.
  2. Place lobster tails on a baking sheet; top with reserved crumb topping. Bake, uncovered, until meat is firm and opaque and crumbs are golden brown, 15-20 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, sprinkle steaks with coarse pepper. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook steaks in remaining oil until meat reaches desired doneness, 4-5 minutes on each side (for medium-rare, a thermometer should read 135째; medium, 140째; medium-well, 145째).
  4. Unwrap herb butter; cut four 1/4-in. slices from log. Place 1 slice on each steak. Serve with lobster. Rewrap remaining butter; refrigerate for 1 week or freeze for up to 3 months.
