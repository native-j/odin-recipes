Sushi sashimi

This round-up showcases some of the tastiest sushi and sashimi recipes around.

Ingredients

  • tuna, sashimi grade, block
  • yellowtail, sashimi grade, fillet
  • salmon, sashimi grade, block
  • wasabi
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Slice tuna and yellowtail into 1/4"-1/3" thick (6 mm-8 mm) pieces.
  2. Slice salmon thinly at a slight angle.
  3. Serve Sashimi on a plate with wasabi along with soy sauce for dipping.
